Follow these methods to test the responsive design:

Method 1: Chrome DevTools (Easiest)

  1. Open entrance.html in Chrome
  2. Press F12 or Ctrl+Shift+I
  3. Click the device toggle button (üì±) or press Ctrl+Shift+M

  2. Start a local server (see below)
  3. On your phone, open browser and go to: http://YOUR_IP:8000/entrance.html

Method 3: Responsive Mode in Firefox

  1. Open in Firefox
  2. Press Ctrl+Shift+M
  3. Choose device or custom dimensions
